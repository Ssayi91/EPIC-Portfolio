About Us

We are a Kenyan based dance company specialized in offering dance training and performance to both the young and grown of age. We are guided by the desire to inspire a generation of professional dancers that will change the perception of the art in our Nation.

Vision

To spring fourth a generation that is passionate in manifesting their gift.

Why Dance

  • My self confidence grows.
  • I develop my concentration.
  • My perseverance skills evolve.
  • I learn to be creative.
  • I improve my social skills.
  • I become strong and healthy.
  • I learn physical expression.
  • I have fun.

Dance

  • Dancing is the hidden language of the Soul
  • If you stumble make it a part of the dance
  • Creative expression of body rhythm
  • Dance is the only art that is a creation in both time and space.
  • Dance is capable of eliciting joy, sorrow, and a wide spectrum of emotions.
