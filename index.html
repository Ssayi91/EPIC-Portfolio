About Us

We are a Kenyan based dance company specialized in offering dance training and performance to both the young and grown of age.
We are guided by the desire to inspire a generation of professional dancers that will change the perception of the art in our Nation.

Vision

To spring fourth a generation that is passionate in manifesting their gift.

Why Dance

  • My self confidence grows
  • I develop my concentration
  • My perseverance skills evolve
  • I learn to be creative
  • I improve my social skills
  • I become strong and healthy
  • I learn physical expression
  • I have fun
